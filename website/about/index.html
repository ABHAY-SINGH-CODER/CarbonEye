About Carbon Eye

Protecting forests through innovative satellite technology

Our Mission

"To preserve Earth's forests by providing real-time, accurate deforestation monitoring through advanced satellite imaging and AI technology, empowering rapid conservation response worldwide."

Our Story

Carbon Eye was founded in 2025 by four college sophomore students who shared a common vision: using technology to combat deforestation in an era where global warming has become normalized.

As we head into a new world where global warming is seen as normal, we believe it's crucial to take a strong stance against environmental destruction. Our team recognized that traditional monitoring methods weren't sufficient to address the scale of deforestation happening worldwide.

What started as a college project has evolved into a comprehensive satellite monitoring system that tracks forest changes across the globe, providing crucial data to support conservation efforts.
